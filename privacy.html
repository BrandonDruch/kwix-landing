Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the email address you submit and a short-lived code. No cookies or tracking beyond Plausible Analytics, which does not collect personal data.

2. How We Use Information

Your email and code are used solely to enable the retrieve-by-code feature. Data is automatically deleted after 5 minutes.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your email address or codes with any third parties.

All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS. Temporary storage is in a secure Redis instance with automatic TTL.

5. Your Choices

You may choose not to use the service. Codes expire automatically; there is no other data to delete.

We may update this policy. Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes.
